Time, Motion & Communication

TMC was one of the most challenging and rewarding electives at CMU . Through gradual tinkering with  After Effects, as well as different themes we explored making both short and long videos that represents various interpretations of motion and narratives with typography, graphic styles and music. I have new found respect for animators, motion graphics artists and film making. After Effects, I’m no longer afraid of you!






I’m always looking for challenging design problems to work on. Feel free to reach out to me if you have an idea that needs an unbiased sounding board or a story to tell.
