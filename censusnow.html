Challenge

Census Now was designed to live both on the screen and on the mobile phone as an app that helps make big data useful to everyone. The project was an outcome of a Prototyping class at CMU, the objective of which was to tinker with Processing to create visualizations of data. The data set could be of any kind.

Data

The data set I worked with was the government spending and debt in America for the last fifteen years. I found data for government spending on defence and education as well as the student loan debt and credit card debt. What was interesting to see was that over the last two years the student loan debt beat credit card debt and the defense expenditure crossed education.

The data can be found at:
US Government Spending
Data.gov

Learning

The visualisation was supported by the context of an app. Census Now was designed as a conceptual app that works in close collaboration with the Government to track trending topics and source data for it to make it accessible for the common public. Tinkering with Processing for the first time was challenging, but once the learning curve was crossed it became a great tool to rethink data visualizations.
